About

This organisztion is an outcome of tremoundous labour and continous efforts.

  • MAY 1996

    Our Humble Beginnings

    As a resident of lucknow since my childhood,I was always inquisitive about the voltage problem our area used to deal with,especially during summers.In order to solve this issue and to make living easier,i started this organization.There were constant tests and efforts made.

  • NOVEMBER 1996

    An Agency is Born

    Apart from the manufacturing of the stabilizers, there was also an aim of facilitating less educated but well skilled people.Hiring of such people was there.An organization evolved which had a team of hard workers,and an idea to help the society.

  • DECEMBER 1996

    Transition to Full Service

    With the constant hard work of our employess we finally managed to manufacture stabilizers for multiple appliances such as refrigerators,air-conditions,photocopy machines and many more.

  • July 1997

    Phase Two Expansion

    We colloborated with 4 renowned distributors of the city-PARVATI ELECTRONICS,S.D. AIR-CONDITIONERS,MANAS ELECTRONICS and SPECTRUM.It was a huge leap for our organization as people started knowing our setup and hence it lead to a lofty expansion of the business.
